How it’s done

We design and build web-based products and platforms using open-source web-based technologies such as Drupal CMS and modern Javascript frameworks. We build secure, flexible and stable systems for our clients. Using Drupal, we empower them to structure, manage and disseminate their communications across multiple channels.

We are able to develop an effective project strategy with you through a process of truth discovery. We utilize this strategy to form a user-story backlog, which formalizes the interaction stories between users and the thing we’re building. These user-stories are the north-star that drive the creation of an information architecture, wireframes, user workflows, UI/UX, code development, and anything else needed for the project.

When working with Therefore on a project, we assign a dedicated multidisciplinary team. This means 100% focus on your project. Close collaboration between you and our team means that we remain focused on building the right thing, the right way.
